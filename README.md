## GDPR and Data Protection

EU General Data Protection Regulation (2016/679) and related data protection frameworks.

## eIDAS and Digital Identity

Electronic Identification, Authentication and Trust Services regulation for cross-border digital identity.

## Electronic Invoicing

Peppol, EN 16931, and the EU e-invoicing directive (2014/55/EU) for cross-border electronic invoicing.

## Payments and Banking

SEPA, PSD2, Open Banking, and the Single Euro Payments Area infrastructure.

## VAT, Customs, and Trade

EU VAT system (VIES, OSS/IOSS), TARIC customs tariffs, CN codes, and Intrastat.

## Open Data and Statistics

Eurostat, EU Open Data Portal, and pan-European statistical and data infrastructure.

## Legal and Legislation

EUR-Lex, ECLI, CELEX, and tools for accessing and processing European legislation.

## Energy and Electricity

ENTSO-E Transparency Platform, ACER, EU ETS, REMIT, and the European energy market.

## Transport and Mobility

European railway interoperability (ERA, ETCS), cross-border transport, and EU mobility frameworks.

## Geospatial and Earth Observation

INSPIRE directive, Copernicus programme, Galileo GNSS, and European geospatial infrastructure.

## Agriculture and Food Safety

Common Agricultural Policy (CAP), IACS, LPIS, EFSA, and EU food safety frameworks.

## Health

European Health Insurance Card (EHIC), European Health Data Space (EHDS), and cross-border healthcare.

## Telecommunications

EU roaming regulation, BEREC, and pan-European telecommunications frameworks.

## Cybersecurity

NIS2 directive, ENISA, EU Cybersecurity Act, and European cyber resilience frameworks.

## AI Regulation

EU AI Act and related frameworks for artificial intelligence governance in Europe.

## Accessibility

European Accessibility Act, EN 301 549, and EU accessibility standards for digital services.

## Environment and Climate

European Environment Agency (EEA), REACH regulation, EU Emissions Trading System, and environmental reporting.

## Finance and Capital Markets

EBA, ECB, ESMA regulations, MiFID II, MiCA, DORA, and European financial market infrastructure.

## Education and Research

ECTS, Erasmus+, Bologna Process, and pan-European education and research infrastructure.

Awesome lists focused on individual European countries.

## Note

This list focuses on open source software that provides **support specifically for Europe** — its institutions, regulations, standards, and cross-border infrastructure. Software specific to a single country belongs in country-specific awesome lists. Software that is global and merely happens to work in Europe is also out of scope.

## Disclaimer

No projects related to pornography, NSFW content, gambling, religion, partisan politics, or any other controversial topic are accepted. This list aims to be a neutral and useful technical resource for the developer community.
